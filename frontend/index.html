Opportunity benchmark validation

These values describe the out-of-fold case-mix benchmark used for group comparisons. Individual error remains substantial; reliability comes from adequate group volume, uncertainty intervals, and calibrated aggregate totals.

Feature decision: Procedure, disposition, charges, and other post-admission fields are intentionally excluded even if they improve apparent accuracy. Including them would leak treatment or outcome information and could hide operational differences. APR-DRG already captures newborn birth-weight bands, so raw birth weight adds little general value.
Cluster audit: Unsupervised clinical clustering found only a weak two-group separation (silhouette 0.194), largely emergency medical versus maternity/newborn/elective care. The tool therefore uses transparent service lines instead of opaque cluster labels.
